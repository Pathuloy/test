As a depressed teenager, I asked myself “What is my purpose on this earth?” Which was followed by self doubt and self loathing. Longing for meaning I read literature by people who found purpose in spite of (or perhaps because of) tremendous pain. One example is the late Claire Wineland who, at age 21 founded a nonprofit to help the families of cystic fibrosis children. Claire found her purpose and it inspired me to find mine. We all have a long way to go and that is what makes this journey exciting. The more we grow the more defined our purpose gets. It is through living with purpose that we will recognize what we want to be remembered for.
